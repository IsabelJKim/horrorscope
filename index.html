What is Horrorscope?

Horrorscope is a service which provides deep psychological analysis of the client's text. The text may consist of any content that has been generated by the client [examples include: fiction, nonfiction, journals, fanfiction, and social media posts]. 

We provide written and oral analysis of the client's text, delivered publicly and/or privately to the client, providing nuanced psychological analysis of the client's mental state in order to give the client greater understanding of their psyche. 

Or to put it simply, "we read you for filth."

 How does Horrorscope work? 

Once you apply for Horrorscope, we will privately contact you through email (or other preferred social media handle) to negotiate the details of your Horrorscope Consultation and Tier package. 

  Is Horrorscope real? 

Horrorscope is about as real as the art of deep freudian psychological analysis, bitcoin, and destiel's canonicity. It's a joke about internet psychics, horoscopes, creativity, and the mortifying ordeal of being known. It is also a service for having your work judged by an internet stranger in exchange for money. 

Horrorscope is run by a real artist writer weird internet guy, Isabel J. Kim. Horrorscope is an offshoot of Wow If True, a podcast about internet culture.
